About Me

My journey into the digital world began with music. I graduated from a music academy and worked as a professional musician in chamber and symphonic orchestras. My daily life was immersed in art, discipline, and creativity.

But one day, a new passion found me — web development. I was fascinated by the idea that, much like music, interfaces can move, inspire, and tell stories. I completed several online courses and one intensive offline course to fully immerse myself in this new field.

Today, I don't just write code — I craft digital interfaces that speak for themselves. My goal is to merge visual aesthetics, technical excellence, and user comfort in every project I create.
